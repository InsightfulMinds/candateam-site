5 Common Medicare Mistakes
Enrolling in Medicare without understanding the rules and deadlines can cause permanent penalties and unnecessary costs.
01
Missing your Initial Enrollment Period (3 months before and after turning 65).
02
Assuming Medicare covers everything — including dental, vision, and long-term care.
03
Confusing Medicare Advantage vs. Supplement and choosing the wrong network.
04
Skipping Part D because you don't take medications right now.
05
Making decisions without professional guidance tailored to your health and budget.
Medicare isn't one-size-fits-all: the best strategy depends on your health, budget, and long-term goals.
