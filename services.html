Our Services

Comprehensive insurance and estate planning for individuals and families

Medicare

Turning 65? Already on Medicare? We help you find the right plan.

🏥

Medicare Advantage (Part C)

All-in-one plans that combine Part A, Part B, and usually Part D. Many include dental, vision, hearing, and fitness benefits — often at $0 premium.

  • HMO & PPO options available
  • Prescription drug coverage included
  • Annual enrollment: Oct 15 — Dec 7
  • OEP: Jan 1 — Mar 31
🛡

Medicare Supplement (Medigap)

Standardized plans (A through N) that cover the gaps in Original Medicare — copays, coinsurance, deductibles, and excess charges.

  • Use any doctor that accepts Medicare
  • No network restrictions
  • Plan G and Plan N most popular
  • Guaranteed issue rights available
💊

Prescription Drug Plans (Part D)

Standalone drug plans for those on Original Medicare + Medigap. We compare formularies to make sure your medications are covered at the lowest cost.

Health Insurance

Coverage for individuals, families, and those not yet eligible for Medicare

❤

Individual & Family Plans

ACA-compliant plans through Covered California and private carriers. We help you find subsidies and the right coverage level for your budget.

🦷

Dental & Vision

Standalone dental and vision plans — PPO and DHMO options. Covers cleanings, exams, crowns, glasses, contacts, and more.

Life Insurance & Final Expense

Protect your family's future and ensure your final wishes are honored

🌱

Life Insurance

Term and whole life policies from top-rated carriers. We help you find the right coverage amount and premium for your situation.

  • Term: 10, 15, 20, 30 year options
  • Whole life: guaranteed premiums for life
  • No-exam options available
🕊

Final Expense

Affordable whole life policies designed to cover funeral costs, medical bills, and other end-of-life expenses — typically $5,000 to $25,000. Simplified underwriting, no medical exam required.

Estate Planning & Living Trust

Protect your assets and make sure your wishes are carried out

🏠

Living Trust

A living trust lets your family avoid probate — the costly, public court process. Your assets pass directly to your beneficiaries, privately and quickly.

  • Avoids probate court
  • Keeps your affairs private
  • Protects assets if you become incapacitated
  • Can be amended at any time
📋

Estate Planning Package

Comprehensive planning that goes beyond a will. We help you set up the documents that protect you and your family.

  • Last Will & Testament
  • Power of Attorney (financial & medical)
  • Healthcare Directive / Living Will
  • Beneficiary designations review

Retirement Planning

Make your money last through retirement

💰

Retirement Income Strategies

Annuities, IRA rollovers, and income planning to make sure you don't outlive your savings. We work with you to create a retirement income plan that fits your lifestyle and goals.
