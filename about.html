About Us

Your neighbors in the Coachella Valley — serving families since 2010

Our Story

Cesar & Associates Health Plan Advisors was founded with a simple mission: help families in the Coachella Valley navigate the confusing world of insurance and estate planning — in a language they understand.

For over 15 years, we've been the go-to resource for Medicare enrollment, health insurance, life insurance, and estate planning in Indio, Palm Springs, Palm Desert, La Quinta, Cathedral City, and the surrounding communities.

We're not a call center. We're not a website with a chatbot. We're real people who sit across the table from you and explain your options in plain English — or Spanish. We take the time to understand your situation, answer your questions, and find solutions that actually fit your life.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Bilingual service — Full service in English and Spanish. No interpreters, no phone trees, no "press 2 for Spanish."
  • Independent agents — We work with multiple carriers, so we can shop the market and find you the best plan — not just push one company's product.
  • One-stop shop — Medicare, health, life, dental, vision, estate planning, and living trusts. One team handles it all.
  • Free consultations — We never charge for appointments. Our job is to help you make the best decision for your family.
  • Local presence — Our office is on Highway 111 in Indio. Walk in, call, or schedule an appointment. We're here.
